About TruthLens

Our Mission

TruthLens is dedicated to combating misinformation in the digital age. We use advanced artificial intelligence to analyze news content and help users distinguish between authentic reporting and fabricated stories.

How It Works

Our system uses a Passive Aggressive Classifier trained on a massive dataset of real and fake news. It analyzes text patterns, linguistic features, and semantic structures to determine the likelihood of an article being authentic.

Technology

Built with Python, scikit-learn, and NLTK for the backend, and a modern HTML5/CSS3 frontend. The AI model achieves high accuracy in detecting fake news patterns.
